Spin Summit 2018 (poster) is the second workshop of the Spin Summit series focusing on the fundamental and applied aspects of spintronics. 

The meeting will take place in August 2018 in Xingyi, Guizhou Province, China, which is far away from noisy metropolitan areas and an ideal location for escaping the heat wave at the height of summer.

The aim of the workshop is to promote effective and mutually beneficial interactions among participants with plenty of formal and informal discussion time. 

The first edition of Spin Summit (2017) was successfully held in Jinggangshan Mt.
