Conference site and accomodation

Venue: Guolong Argyle Grand Hotel (国龙雅阁大酒店)
Address: Xingyi City, Guizhou Province, China (Baidu map, Google map)

Transportation

By Air: Xingyi Wanfenlin Airport is about 9 km from the Hotel. There are flights connecting to Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Hangzhou etc. 

By Rail: The Xingyi Railway Station is about 18 km from the Xingyi City. The station has daily overnight trains connecting to Beijing and Shanghai, as well as many other major cities in China.

You may use ctrip.com to look up the flight/train schedule or book the tickets. 

Weather

The weather at Xingyi in August is very pleasant. The average low/high temperature is 18ºC/27ºC (65ºF/80ºF). Ideal place for escaping the heat wave at the height of summer.
