Spin Summit 2017 is the first workshop of the Spin Summit series focusing on the fundamental and applied aspects of spintronics. 

The meeting will take place in August 2017 in the scenic mountain of Jinggangshan, Jiangxi Province, China, which is far away from noisy metropolitan areas and an ideal location for escaping the heat wave at the height of summer.

The aim of the workshop is to promote effective and mutually beneficial interactions among participants with plenty of formal and informal discussion time.
